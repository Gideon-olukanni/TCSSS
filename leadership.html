Leadership

Two Systems, One School

Tomia is run by two systems working together: its administration, and a student-led government chosen each academic year.

School Administration
ST
Mrs Sekinat Bolanle Teju-Faniyi
Principal

Welcome to Tomia Community Senior Secondary School. Every student who passes through our gates is here to build two things at once: a mind capable of excellence, and a character strong enough to carry it well.

We hold our students to high standards because we believe they can meet them — in the classroom, in their conduct, and in how they represent this school beyond it.

— Mrs S. B. Teju-Faniyi, Principal

Principals Through the Years
Student Leadership

Each academic year, Tomia's student body is led by a Student Executive Council, headed by a Senior Prefect (Boy) and Senior Prefect (Girl). Together with other appointed prefects, they hold real responsibility for school discipline, order, and student affairs throughout the year.

GO
Gideon Olukanni
Senior Prefect (Boy)

As Senior Prefect, part of my role is to represent this school well — and that includes giving it a presence beyond our gates. This website was built to do exactly that: to give Tomia Community Senior Secondary School a voice and an address online.

To every student here: excellence isn't reserved for a few of us. It's a standard we hold together, in class and out of it.

— Gideon Olukanni, Senior Prefect
